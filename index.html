Have you ever wondered what is happening in your neighborhood ?

Have you ever wondered how you can discover people in your neighborhood ?

You can now.

VahChit is a platform where you - the awesome person - can upload short videos.

These videos are tagged with GPS co-ordinates so that other VahChit users can discover your neighborhood when they are near by.

You can also find other VahChit users who are near to your location and follow them.

Features:

  • Unlimited uploads.
  • Search videos based on location, descriptive tags or a combination of two.
  • Discover fellow users near you and follow them.
  • Interact with fellow users via likes and commenting.
  • Receive notifications when someone likes your video or comments on it.
  • Share your favorite VahChit's with your friends.
